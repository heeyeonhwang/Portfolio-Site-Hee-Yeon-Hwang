Ink Tank

Getting the perfect tattoo is a challenge in itself, from finding design inspirations, to choosing a great artist to make your design come to life. Ink Tank provides users a filtering feature through categories such as specialties, prices, and reviews, feature to comparing artists and ability booking sessions easily.

  • Process: User Research, Wire-framing, Prototyping, User Testing
  • Tools: Design Thinking, Material Design, Figma, Adobe XD

Flash

People want to be able to quickly learn new words but don’t have the time to put aside to study regularly. They also want to maximize their down time to study while multitasking, such as doing chores, but don’t have a method to do so. Flash was created to focuses on users’ needs to study words in multiple methods with visual aids and examples in order to balance their multifaceted lives.

  • Process: Competitive Analysis, User Interviews, Wire-framing, Prototyping, Usability Research
  • Tools: Design Thinking, Protts
